SKOL NETWORK WEEKLY BLOG

NFL Week 2 brings a quick turnaround for the Minnesota Vikings as they head to Philadelphia to take on the Eagles for a matchup on Thursday Night Football! After a lackluster performance against the Buccaneers, there are many ways the team can improve. The offense lacked consistency as it seemed second year head coach and offensive play caller Kevin O'Connell could not find a rhythm. When it mattered most in the 4th quarter the team struggled to move the ball and was forced to punt away their chance to win the game. The defense showed a level of aggression that we had not seen at all last year under previous defensive coordinator Ed Donatell, however the Brian Flores led defense still struggled to get off the field at times as the consistent blitzing looks caused problems defending the quick passing game. As we look ahead to the Eagles, the task does not get any easier. The Vikings will catch a break knowing there are two Eagles defenders -- Pro Bowl cornerback James Bradberry and Starting Safety Reed Blankenship -- who will be out along with starting running back Kenneth Gainwell. It will take a tall task for the Vikings to come out of Philadelphia with a 1-1 record.
